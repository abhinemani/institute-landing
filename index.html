Coming Soon: CivicTech.Institute

an online academy designed to level up your skills for good (government)

The digital government movement has lasted over a decade, but still there are too many current or prospective public servants wanting more training in how to get the job done. The Academy will be a practical, hands-on certification program on the essential skills for modern governance. Whether you're looking to get into service or grow professionally in your current role, the Academy will have something for you with courses designed and taught by leaders in the field.
